The Lovers show us that we must make choices in how we show up in our relationships. That may be choosing between partners or putting a loved one's needs before our own. Every choice has consequences! Reversed this card can show us we are out of sync with ourselves and the world around us. This can be frustrating and lead to choices we may not usually make.
